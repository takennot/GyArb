FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why won't the computer start?
  • The whole computer or a specific part or component doesn't turn on:
    Nothing turns on or the PC is not responding/reacting, some of the parts are getting power and showing activity, while the other ones are being completely powerless (for example some fans are spinning and some aren't), or a component is not turning on (for example, if provided lights on a component are not shining, "beeping" occurs but there is no image on the display, etc.
    1. The computer/certain part/component is probably not getting power. Check if the PSU switch is on, or any problems with the outlet.
    2. Check if every cable is connected and seated properly. A certain cable or part could also be damaged.
    3. There could be a software problem, where it is installed incorectly or the BIOS is not supported/updated.
  • How should I proceed if my hands/fingers are too big to reach/grab/handle a part of the computer in the process of building it?

  • Use tools that will allow you to manage that part, such as tweezers. Alternatively, you could ask for a help from someone with smaller fingers.

  • What to do if my PC is not starting?

  • One of the reasons why your PC is not starting might be the RAM stick(s). Check if it/they are seated properly and try to push them fully if they aren't.

  • My storage device (SSD/HDD) is not showing, what should I do?

  • Storage device isn't showing because either the cables are connected badly, or because it has been assigned with a wrong letter in the operative system. To show the storage device in the OS - check if cables are properly connected and then check which letter do they have in Disk Management

  • Some parts are not fitting inside the case, why?

  • You probably didn't check the dimensions of the components before ordering them. Or you didn't check the dimensions inside the case. What you could do is to either get a refund and order a different component/case, either try to modify your case by removing some of it's parts (NOT recommended)
